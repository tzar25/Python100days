Cinnamon Roll

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups of bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix milk with
    • yeast
    • sugar
    • eggs
  2. Melt butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer it to large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After it doubled in size, roll it to a rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the filling onto the dough and roll it into a swiss roll.
  9. Cut the roll to 3cm sections and place flat into baking tray.
  10. Preheat to 180Â°C, then bake for 20-25minutes until lightly brown.
